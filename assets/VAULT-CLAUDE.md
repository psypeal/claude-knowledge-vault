
## Knowledge Vault

This project has a knowledge vault at `.vault/`. Use `/vault:*` commands or natural language.

**Commands**: `/vault:init`, `/vault:ingest`, `/vault:compile`, `/vault:lint`, `/vault:cleanup`, `/vault:query`, `/vault:process`, `/vault:collect`, `/vault:setup-sources`, `/vault:status`, `/vault:agent-reset`

The vault is not consulted unless explicitly asked. All wiki content is maintained by Claude — do not edit `.vault/wiki/` or `.vault/agent.md` files manually.
